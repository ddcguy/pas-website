Performance Automation Solutions is a growing team of engineers, technicians, and system integrators who take pride in doing complex work well. If you're serious about building automation, we want to hear from you.

Who We Are

A Team Built on Technical Excellence

Founded in 2009, Performance Automation Solutions has grown from three people to a team of 50+ professionals executing projects across military, higher education, government, commercial, and healthcare sectors throughout California.

We're a woman-owned small business with deep roots in the mechanical contracting world. That background shapes our culture: we value tradespeople who understand how buildings actually work, engineers who sweat the details, and people who take accountability seriously.

What We Do

Real Projects. Real Impact.

Our work spans new construction design-build, systems integration, and long-term service agreements — meaning our team works across the full lifecycle of a building's controls infrastructure. No two days look the same.
