Services / Service & Maintenance

Systems Kept
Running. Precisely.

Building automation systems are only as good as their uptime. Performance Automation Solutions offers the full spectrum of service and maintenance support — from emergency response to structured preventive care programs.

Service Calls

Responsive. Skilled. On-Site.

When something goes wrong with your building automation system, every hour of downtime has a cost — in energy waste, occupant discomfort, or compliance risk. Performance Automation Solutions dispatches experienced technicians who know building controls at the system level, not just the device level.

Our service team is equipped to diagnose and resolve issues across a wide range of BAS platforms, including Distech Controls, Siemens, Johnson Controls, Alerton, ALC, and others. We don't just fix the symptom — we identify the root cause.

Preventive Maintenance

Planned Care. Longer Life.

Unplanned failures are almost always more expensive than preventive care. A structured maintenance program from Performance Automation Solutions protects your building automation investment, extends equipment life, and surfaces emerging issues before they become critical failures.

Our preventive maintenance agreements are customized to your system's complexity, age, and operational requirements. We document findings, track trends, and provide clear reporting so facilities teams always know the health of their systems.

Scheduled inspections
Sensor calibration
Software updates
Controller testing
Network health checks
Trending & analytics review
Service Agreements

Flexible Programs for Every Facility

Whether you manage a single building or a portfolio of facilities, Performance Automation Solutions can structure a service agreement that matches your operational needs and budget. From time-and-material service to fully inclusive annual contracts, we scale our support to fit your requirements.

Our service agreements provide priority scheduling, discounted labor rates, documented maintenance histories, and a single point of contact who knows your system. It's the kind of partnership that turns a vendor relationship into a long-term operational advantage.
